The Game will start after pressing any key. It will show a sequence by flashing a square.

After each flash click on the square that lit up plus all the previous ones to reproduce the sequence shown by the game.

Example:

Level 1: flash on the red square -> press on the red one.

Level 2: flash on the green square -> press on the red, then on the green one.

Level 3: flash on the yellow square -> press on the red, on the green and then on the yellow...

Level 4: ...
